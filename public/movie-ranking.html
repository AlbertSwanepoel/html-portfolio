Albert's best three movies of all time

Three movies you have to see.

Shawshank Redemption

Who knew Stephen King could write brilliant but inspiring prison drama?

Baby Driver

You cannot be other than mesmerised by brilliant music syncopation.
And the opening car chase will remain the benchmark for others to be measured against for years to come!

Pan's Labyrinth

Mexican all-round maestro Guillermo del Toro's masterpiece of good vs. evil, achieved through a brilliant and fantastic journey of a young girl - a mesmerising fantasy through a mystical labyrinth.
